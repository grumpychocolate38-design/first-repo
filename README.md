# Reddit Marketing for B2B SaaS - Expert Research Project

## Project Overview

I researched 10 industry experts in Reddit marketing to understand proven strategies for B2B SaaS growth. This repository contains curated insights from LinkedIn posts, YouTube transcripts, and community analysis.

## Why Reddit for B2B SaaS?

Reddit is the 4th most visited website globally, yet most B2B SaaS companies treat it as an afterthought. The platform has 430M+ monthly users discussing problems your customers are trying to solve. Unlike traditional advertising, Reddit audiences are actively seeking solutions, not avoiding ads.

## Expert Selection Criteria

I selected 10 experts based on:
- **Real experience** (not just theory) - people actively using Reddit for growth
- **Proven results** - demonstrated case studies and metrics
- **Diverse perspectives** - mix of different B2B approaches and strategies
- **Active contributors** - recently sharing (not outdated advice)

See `research/sources.md` for full expert list.

## Key Findings

### Finding 1: Micro-Communities > Generic Hubs
Most brands target r/SaaS or r/Entrepreneur. Smart B2B companies target niche subreddits (5k-20k members) where their ICP congregates. Result: 5-10x better engagement.

### Finding 2: Authenticity is the Currency
Reddit detects inauthenticity instantly. Account age, karma, and posting history matter more than follower count. One authentic voice > 10 fake accounts.

### Finding 3: The AI Flywheel
ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude pull from Reddit to generate answers. High-karma comments influence AI recommendations for years.
