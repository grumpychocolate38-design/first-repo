## Key Insights
- **The "Niche" Multiplier**: The most effective Reddit marketing happens in smaller, highly specialized subreddits where the target audience congregates, rather than in generic, high-traffic "hub" subreddits like r/SaaS or r/Entrepreneur.
- **Tone Alignment**: Success depends on mirroring the specific voice and tone of the target community. Marketing that "reads like marketing" is immediately rejected; content must sound like it belongs to the community ecosystem.

## My Takeaways for Reddit B2B SaaS Strategy
- **Prioritize Micro-Communities**: Stop targeting massive, generic subreddits. Invest time in identifying and engaging with smaller, high-intent communities where your specific ICP is already discussing problems.
- **Optimize for Timing**: Don't treat posting as an arbitrary task. Analyze when your specific target audience is most active and ensure your content hits at those peak windows to maximize visibility.
