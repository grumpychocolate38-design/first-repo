## Post Content

If you want to find customers in Reddit communities:

1. Go to reddit .com/best/communities/1/

2. Select the biggest communities in your niche

3. Start your post with the following sentence to avoid downvotes: 
”With the hope to provide value first and ask for help later, here are… (provide valuable list or info related to your industry)"

4. At the end, add a CTA like this: 
“Is this valuable to you and if so how could it be better? {Insert your product}

5. You’ll get much more attention on your posts

## Key Insights
- **Subreddit research is critical** - don't just post anywhere
- **Community-first approach** beats direct selling
- **Value-add content** is the gateway to credibility
- **Analytics matter** - track engagement and adapt
